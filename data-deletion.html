Your Right to Delete Your Data

At Wayon, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You can request the deletion of your data at any time using any of the methods described below.

Option 1: Remove the App from Your Facebook Account

If you signed up using Facebook Login, you can remove Wayon from your Facebook account by following these steps:

1

Log in to your Facebook account and go to Settings ‚Üí Apps and Websites.

2

Find "wayon" in the list of active apps.

3

Click on the app, then click "Remove".

4

Check the box that says "Delete all posts, photos and videos on Facebook that wayon may have published on your behalf", then confirm.

This will revoke Wayon's access to your Facebook data. To also delete the data stored on our servers, please use Option 2 below.
